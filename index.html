Opportunity

Demand is surging. Licensed supply lags.

  • Parents of infants & toddlers struggle to find safe, affordable, licensed childcare in Ontario as demand outpaces supply.
  • Unlicensed providers operate with limited oversight, may expose families to added legal or safety risks, and often charge more because they’re ineligible for public subsidies.
  • Families of infants/toddlers favour smaller, home-based settings; licensing unlocks subsidies and trust.
  • Ontario’s CWELCC agreement brings multi-year funding & expansion targets—prime timing to build capacity.

Illustrative trendline: demand > supply → opportunity for licensed home-based capacity.
